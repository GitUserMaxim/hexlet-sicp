<p>Write a procedure 
<code>triples</code>
 that takes three infinite streams, 
<code>S</code>
, 
<code>T</code>
, and 
<code>U</code>
, and produces the stream of triples 
<code>(Si, Tj, Uk)</code>
 such that 
<code>i ≤ j ≤ k</code>
. Use 
<code>triples</code>
 to generate the stream of all Pythagorean triples of positive integers, i.e., the triples 
<code>(i, j, k)</code>
 such that 
<code>i ≤ j</code>
 and 
<code>i² + j² = k²</code>
.
</p>
